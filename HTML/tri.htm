Tribute to Indian Freedom Fighters

Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, commonly known as Mahatma Gandhi, was a key leader in India's struggle for independence through non-violent civil disobedience.

Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose was a prominent Indian nationalist leader who attempted to gain India's independence by force with the help of Nazi Germany and Imperial Japan during World War II.

Jawaharlal Nehru

Jawaharlal Nehru, the first Prime Minister of India, was a central figure in Indian politics before and after independence. He played a key role in the Indian National Congress.

Remembering and saluting the great leaders who fought for India's freedom.
